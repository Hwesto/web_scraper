About me

Therapy that meets you where you are

I'm Sarah Bennett, a qualified counsellor based in Maidstone, Kent. I work with adults who are going through a difficult time and want to understand themselves more deeply. My approach is gentle, collaborative and tailored to you — there's no script, and no pressure to have the right words.

I draw on a person-centred and integrative approach, which simply means I adapt the way we work to suit what you need, rather than fitting you into one method. Many of my clients come to me feeling stuck, anxious or exhausted, and leave with a clearer sense of who they are and what they want.

Sessions are completely confidential. Everything you bring stays between us, within the limits of the BACP Ethical Framework I work to.

How I can help

Support for what you're carrying

People come to counselling for all sorts of reasons. These are some of the areas I work with most often — but if what you're facing isn't listed, please still get in touch.

    Racing thoughts, panic, overthinking and that constant feeling of being on edge.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

How much does counselling cost?

Sessions are £55 for 50 minutes. The first 15-minute phone consultation is free, so you can see whether we feel like a good fit before booking anything.

Do you offer online counselling as well as in person?

Yes. I offer face-to-face sessions in Maidstone, Kent, and secure online video sessions for clients across Kent and the wider UK.

Are you a qualified, registered counsellor?

Yes — I'm a registered member of the BACP and work to its Ethical Framework, with regular clinical supervision and full professional insurance.

How many sessions will I need?

There's no fixed number. Some people come for a handful of sessions to work through something specific; others value longer-term support. We'll review regularly and you're always in control.

Is everything I say confidential?

Yes. What you share stays private, within the limits of the BACP Ethical Framework — which I'll explain fully in our first session.
